A Good Sleep Starts from a Silk Duvet

Research shows that poor sleep has immediate negative effects on your overall health and makes you prone to serious medical conditions, such as obesity, heart disease, high blood pressure and diabetes.

Silk duvets can help you get a good night’s sleep and keep you warm when cold and cool when hot. A silk duvet comes with many benefits to ensure a comfort sleep.

Breathability Features - Unlike down or synthetic fibers, silk has a unique quality that allows your duvet to breathe. A silk duvet helps you to maintain a comfortable body temperature throughout the night, meaning you will not overheat or feel cold. Silk duvet allows you to enjoy a consistent body temperature over the night to ensure your total comfort. This is especially important if you often overheat at night or are experiencing symptoms of menopause.

Hypoallergenic Benefits - Allergies may be caused by a variety of factors, including mold, dust mites, chemicals, or fungus. Silk is a natural product that features a defensive barrier against all other natural enemies, such as dust mites or fungus. Furthermore, producing quality silk does not involve any type of chemicals, but soaking in water, heat, or steam. Therefore, silk duvets are naturally hypoallergenic, which means that you will not sneeze and toss all night. You will get a good night’s sleep. 

Comfortable Feeling - A silk touch describes best how silk duvets feel in comparison with traditional duvets. They feel light and comfortable on the body, while warmth is there to pamper you in those cold nights. Silk duvets have a very different feel compared to the traditional silk duvets; the thousand layers of silk floss batting makes them the preferred choice among those who wish to sleep comfortably every night.

Soothing for the Joints- Many people suffered from all sorts of joint pains. When traditional duvets may be too heavy and irritating sensitive joints, the silk duvet benefits eliminate the single points of pressure to your joints therefore you will be able to get a better sleep.

Comfort for the Skin - Silk fiber is the finest natural fiber that comes from silkworms which is similar in structure with the human skin. Silk duvet allows your skin to remain moisturized during sleep specially to benefit those who suffer from dry skin or eczema symptoms.  Silk duvets help absorb moisture and rejuvenate the skin while sleeping comfortably.
